Know More About Us

Our factory has more than 10 years experiences of producing PVC products. Till now we have PVC ceiling and wall panel, PVC skirting and SPC/RVP flooring. We totally have production lines.

We are a modern factory which develops new products, produces and sells advanced products. Our factory always focus on product’s quality. We have best extruding and grooving machines in China and our products are already certified by CE.

Every year we attend many different exhibitions all over the world. For example, we attend Dubai Big 5 and Dubai surfaces. We attend Canton Fairtwice every year. Through attending these professional exhibitions, we only sell our products all over the world, but also we improve our technology and version with many customer’s help.

Our factory supply customers best products with most advanced technology, good quality and service and reasonable price. We hope win more customer’s appreciation and cooperation in the future with our best products and service.
